About Us

Velo Quirk was born out of hearing the constant question from friends outside of the biking community ask, "I want to get a bicycle. Which one would you recommend I get?" Only to get intimidated by a bike shop or be guided to a specific brand only for marketing reasons.

Velo Quirk is designed to arm those new to biking with the information necessary to make a educated decision on the appropriate fun machine to purchase.
